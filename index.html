Maccabi Haifa

Football club Maccabi Haifa

The official website

Maccabi Haifa is an Israeli football club from the city of Haifa, playing in the Israeli Premier League.
The club was established in 1913, and since 1992 it has been run by businessman Yaakov Shachar, who serves as the club's president.
The club also includes youth teams, boys and children, and football schools. The club is a partner in the organization of European clubs.
